Mobile Marketing Presentation

  • Mobile marketing is an advertising activity that uses mobile devices, such as text promos and apps via push notifications.
  • Mobile marketing audiences are grouped by behaviors and not by demographics.
  • Mobile marketing is a subset of mobile advertising.
  • Marketing faces privacy issues related to data collection.
  • Mobile marketing is much more affordable than traditional marketing on television and radio.

Why Use Mobile Marketing?

  • Easy Access

    Accessibility plays a big role in the success of mobile marketing. It allows you to reach your audience anywhere and anytime.
    Also, mobile marketing is the fastest way to communicate with consumers and get your message out right now.

  • Cost Effectiveness

    When compared to traditional marketing techniques, mobile marketing is significantly cheaper.
    Optimizing your website for mobile use or sending text messages is less expensive than many traditional marketing techniques.

  • Instant Transaction

    Mobile is an immediate medium. Every element of mobile marketing is like point of sale (POS) advertising. Any Mobile site appears on local search listings of consumers whose ad may be placed strategically on social media. Thus, mobile marketing enables instant transactions.

  • Localization

    We can utilize localization via mobile marketing.
    Geofencing is one example. When someone with your mobile app goes into the area, they get a particular push notification.
    Localization, though, is also useful to ecommerce businesses.
